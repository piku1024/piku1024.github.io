The man walks for a short distance before he reaches the

riverfront. There, he sits down, waiting for something.

 

A crowd slowly forms all along the riverfront. After about

thirty minutes, you hear a buzz and look to your left and spot

six cars travelling slowly a few kilometres away. The crowd

seems to be cheering for them.

Next
